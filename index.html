About

Dr Somesh Virmani is a fellowship trained paediatric orthopaedic surgeon. He has compeleted his two years fellowship in paediatric orthopaedics at one of the most renowned institute of the Asia Pacific region – Christian Medical College, Vellore. The Paediatric Orthoapedic Unit at CMC, Vellore, the largest unit in India, has rich clinical material. It is here that Dr. Virmani got exposure to all the nuances of this speciality and learnt the art of examining and treating these kids and teenagers with various orthopaedic issues.

He believes in a strongly ethical practice that is based on latest evidence according to best and latest international standards and scientific literature. He practices paediatric orthopaedics exclusively and is completely focused on treating bones and joints problems in kids. He has established a successful practice in a short span of 3 years and is now working in Delhi NCR region. If you would like to consult Dr. Virmani, please click here to make an appointment.

Dr. Virmani regularly speaks at various national and state conferences and meetings including Paediatric Orthopaedic Society Of India Annual Conference (POSICON), Indian Orthaopaedic Association Annual Conference (IOACON), Delhi Orthopaedic Association Annual Conference (DOACON), PGIMS Rohtak, and more. He is a member of many prestigious orthopaedic societies and organizations including the following:

  • Paediatric Orthopaedic Society of India (POSI)
  • Indian Orthopaedic Association (IOA)
  • Delhi Orthopaedic Association (DOA)
  • Indian Association of Cerebral Palsy (IACP)
  • Société Internationale de Chirurgie Orthopédique et de Traumatologie (SICOT)
  • Asian Association of Dynamic Osteosynthesis (AADO)
